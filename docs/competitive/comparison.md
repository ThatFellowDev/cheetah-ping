# Competitive Comparison: Cheetah Ping vs Visualping vs UptimeRobot

## Pricing Comparison

| | Cheetah Ping | Visualping | UptimeRobot |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free tier** | $0 (2 monitors, 24h) | Not listed (limited) | $0 (50 monitors, 5min) |
| **Cheapest paid** | $9/mo (10 monitors, 15min) | $50/mo (5 pages, 10K checks) | $7/mo (10-50 monitors, 60s) |
| **Mid tier** | $19/mo (50 monitors, 5min) | $100/mo (200 pages, 20K checks) | $29/mo (100 monitors, 60s) |
| **Top tier** | $49/mo (25 monitors, 1min) | $3,000+/yr enterprise | $54/mo (200+ monitors, 30s) |

### Key pricing insight
- **Visualping is expensive.** $50/mo for just 5 pages. Their Personal plan is very limited.
- **UptimeRobot is generous on free.** 50 monitors free at 5-min intervals. Hard to beat on free tier.
- **UptimeRobot's paid is cheap.** $7/mo for 60-second monitoring.
- **Our positioning:** Cheaper than Visualping, more focused on change detection than UptimeRobot (which is primarily uptime monitoring, not content change detection).

## Where We Win

1. **Price vs Visualping** - We're 80% cheaper. $9/mo vs $50/mo for similar features.
2. **AI setup** - We auto-analyze URLs with Groq. Nobody else does this.
3. **Simplicity** - One screen, paste URL, done. Their UIs are cluttered.
4. **Speed messaging** - "Every minute" on Ultra vs Visualping's 2-min max.
5. **Consumer focus** - We speak to job seekers, apartment hunters. They speak to compliance teams.

## Where We Lose

1. **UptimeRobot free tier** - 50 monitors free is very generous. We offer 2.
2. **No screenshot diffs** - Visualping's core differentiator. Visual before/after is compelling.
3. **No Slack/SMS** - Both competitors have multi-channel notifications.
4. **No social proof** - They have millions of users. We have zero.
5. **No status pages** - UptimeRobot's status pages are a big draw.
6. **No JS rendering** - Visualping can monitor SPAs. We can't.
7. **No mobile app** - UptimeRobot has Android + iOS apps.

## What to Steal / Improve

### Immediate (next sprint)
1. **Increase free tier to 5 monitors** - Compete better with UptimeRobot's 50 free
2. **Add a feature comparison table to landing page** - Show we beat Visualping on price
3. **Add "Trusted by X users" counter** - Even if small, social proof matters

### Near-term (v2)
1. **Slack + Discord notifications** - Table stakes for paid users
2. **AI change summaries** - Groq can summarize diffs in plain English. Visualping charges extra for AI.
3. **Public monitor pages** - Like UptimeRobot's status pages but for change tracking

### Long-term (v3)
1. **Screenshot diffs** - Use Playwright to capture before/after screenshots
2. **Chrome extension** - One-click "Watch this page" from any website
3. **Mobile app** - React Native or Expo
