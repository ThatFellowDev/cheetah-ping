Cheetah Ping offers four plans to fit different monitoring needs.

## Plans

| Feature | Free | Starter | Pro | Ultra |
|---------|------|---------|-----|-------|
| Price | $0/mo | $9/mo | $19/mo | $49/mo |
| Monitors | 5 | 10 | 50 | 50 |
| Fastest interval | 24 hours | 15 minutes | 5 minutes | 1 minute |
| Change history | 7 days | 30 days | 90 days | 180 days |
| Email alerts | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Webhook alerts | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| AI analysis | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |

All plans include AI-powered monitor setup, visual diffs, and email alerts.

## Upgrading

To upgrade your plan:
1. Go to **Settings** in your dashboard
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Choose your new plan

Upgrades take effect immediately. You get prorated credit for the remaining time on your current billing cycle.

## Downgrading

When you downgrade:
- Your new plan limits take effect at the next billing cycle
- If you have more monitors than your new plan allows, you will need to pause or delete some before the change takes effect
- Change history beyond your new plan's retention period will be automatically cleaned up

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Settings page. After cancellation:
- Your account reverts to the Free plan at the end of the current billing period
- Your monitors remain active (up to the Free plan limit)
- Change history beyond 7 days will be purged after the transition

## Payment

Payments are processed securely through Stripe. We accept all major credit cards. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
