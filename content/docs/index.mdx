Welcome to the Cheetah Ping documentation. Here you will find everything you need to get started with website change monitoring.

## What is Cheetah Ping?

Cheetah Ping is a website change monitoring platform. Paste a link, tell it what to watch, and get alerted the moment something changes. Track price drops, restocks, job postings, content updates, and more on any website.

## Key features

- **Three watch modes**: monitor entire pages, specific keywords, or precise CSS selectors
- **AI-powered setup**: paste a URL and our AI suggests the best monitoring configuration
- **Flexible alerts**: get notified via email, Slack, or Discord webhooks
- **Fast checks**: monitoring intervals from every minute to daily, depending on your plan
- **Visual diffs**: see exactly what changed with highlighted before/after comparisons
