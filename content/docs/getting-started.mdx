## 1. Sign up

Head to [cheetahping.com](https://cheetahping.com) and click "Get started free." Sign in with your email using a magic link. No password needed.

## 2. Create a monitor

From the dashboard, click **New Monitor**. Paste the URL of any webpage you want to track.

Our AI will analyze the page and suggest:
- A descriptive label for your monitor
- The best watch mode (page, keyword, or selector)
- A recommended check frequency

You can accept the suggestion or customize any setting before saving.

## 3. Get alerted

When Cheetah Ping detects a change, you will receive an alert via your configured notification channel (email by default). Each alert includes:
- A summary of what changed
- A visual diff showing the before and after
- A direct link to view the full change log

## What to monitor

Common use cases include:
- **Price drops**: watch a product page for price changes
- **Job postings**: monitor a careers page for new listings
- **Restocks**: track an out-of-stock product until it comes back
- **Competitor changes**: watch a competitor's pricing or feature page
- **Government updates**: monitor regulatory or legal pages for policy changes
